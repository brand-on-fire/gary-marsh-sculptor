Gary Marsh

Artists Statement

My passion is the exploration and study of form, intertwining shapes found in nature with those of mans architectural influence; combining forms unscathed by mans hands, with forms created entirely by man.

Currently my sculptures are focused on large clay forms, morphing the line between architecture and the human body.

My recent paintings address and explore the painful impression we have made, and continue to inflict on this earth.
